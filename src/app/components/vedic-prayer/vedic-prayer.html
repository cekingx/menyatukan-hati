Sacred Prayer

Om Sahana Vavatu

ॐ सह नाववतु

सह नौ भुनक्तु

सह वीर्यं करवावहै

तेजस्विनावधीतमस्तु

मा विद्विषावहै

ॐ शान्तिः शान्तिः शान्तिः

"May we be protected together, may we be nourished together, may we work together with great vigor, may our study be enlightening and fruitful, may we never quarrel with each other. Om, peace, peace, peace."

ॐ
